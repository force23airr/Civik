# DashGuard — Core Value Propositions

---

## 1. Citizen → Government, Finally Working

The #1 frustration for drivers is reporting a pothole or broken streetlight and hearing nothing back. DashGuard closes that loop — your report goes directly into the city's 311 system or lands in a municipal worker's inbox automatically. No more calling a hotline that puts you on hold.

---

## 2. Get Paid to Be a Good Citizen

You photograph an illegally parked car blocking a fire hydrant. A cop approves it. You get $7.50. That's new. No app does this at scale. It turns every phone into a revenue opportunity for safety-conscious people — especially gig drivers who are already on the road all day.

---

## 3. Gig Driver as the Distribution Engine

Uber/DoorDash/Lyft drivers drive 8–12 hours a day through every neighborhood. They see more potholes, illegal parking, and dangerous drivers than anyone. DashGuard gives them a reason to report it (bounties + credits) while they're already driving. This is the viral growth vector — TikTok content writes itself.

---

## 4. Dashcam as Evidence, Not Just Storage

Most people's dashcam footage sits on an SD card and gets overwritten. DashGuard turns that footage into actionable evidence — plate detection runs automatically, footage gets timestamped and hashed for chain of custody, and it goes directly to police or municipalities. The footage finally does something.

---

## 5. The Aggregation Layer Nobody Built

SeeClickFix, FixMyStreet, PotholePro — they all failed because they're city-by-city, require government contracts, and have zero consumer presence. DashGuard flips it: build the consumer audience first (viral, bounties, TikTok), then municipalities come to claim their queue because there are already reports waiting for them. You don't need the city's permission to start.

---

## The One-Liner

> **DashGuard is the app that pays drivers to fix their city** — every pothole reported, every illegal parker ticketed, every bad driver flagged goes somewhere real.

---

## Why This Compounds

The data network effects build over time:

- More drivers → more reports
- More reports → more government adoption
- More government adoption → more credibility
- More credibility → more drivers

The bounty system is what makes people open the app unprompted. That's the moat.
